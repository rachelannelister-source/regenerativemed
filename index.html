Treatment Options

PRP Therapy

Uses your body’s own platelets to support healing, reduce inflammation, and promote recovery in injured or damaged tissue. This treatment is commonly used for joint pain, tendon injuries, and other musculoskeletal conditions as part of a personalized care plan.

Stem Cell Treatments

Stem cell-based treatments are designed to support your body’s ability to heal and recover. They are often considered for joint pain and certain injuries as a minimally invasive option before more advanced procedures.

Joint Injections

Image-guided injections used to deliver medication directly to the affected joint. These treatments are designed to reduce inflammation and improve function based on your diagnosis and evaluation.

Questions & Answers

What is regenerative medicine?

Regenerative medicine uses biologic treatments, such as platelet-rich plasma (PRP) and other cell-based therapies, to support the body’s natural healing response and improve function in damaged or injured tissues.

Who is a candidate?

Candidates are typically those dealing with joint pain, injuries, or chronic conditions who are looking for non-surgical treatment options. The best way to determine if regenerative medicine is right for you is through a thorough evaluation, including your medical history, imaging, and diagnosis.

Are regenerative medicine treatments surgical?

Most regenerative medicine treatments are minimally invasive and are not considered traditional surgery. They are typically performed using image-guided injections designed to precisely target the affected area. Because they do not involve large incisions or extended recovery times, these treatments are often explored before more invasive surgical options, depending on your condition and overall treatment plan.
